Crystal Game

The name of the Game is Crystal Collector, and you will be given random numbers at the start of the game. There's three crystals below, you will have a total number and by clicking on a crystal you will be adding an amount of points to your total score. The value of each crystal changes everytime the page is refreshed or the game is restarted. In order for you to win the game the total score has to match the random number, or you will lose the game.
